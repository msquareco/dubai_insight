Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dubai a good place to invest in property?

Yes. Dubai offers tax-free rental income, high ROI (up to 8–10%), and investor-friendly regulations.

What documents are required for foreigners to invest?

Passport copy, proof of funds, and signed sales agreements. No local sponsor or visa is required to buy property.

Is it safe and legal to invest as an NRI?

Absolutely. Dubai is one of the safest and most regulated markets for overseas investors.

What are AML (Anti-Money Laundering) guidelines in Dubai?

Dubai enforces strict KYC norms and AML checks through banks and real estate brokers to ensure safe, legal transactions.

Can I get residency with property purchase?

Yes. Property investment above AED 750,000 may make you eligible for a renewable investor visa.

What types of properties are trending now?

Off-plan luxury, short-term rental homes, branded residences, and community living apartments are in high demand.
